Titans Circuit

Instructions:

Take turns placing titans on the outermost circuit first and then place the remaining titans on the inner node. When the unlocked circuit is fully filled, the inner circuit gets unlockedOnce all titans are placed, players take turns moving one titan at a time to an adjacent node along connected edges

Scoring Conditions:

An edge is controlled when both its connected nodes are occupied by the same player. Points equal to the edge's weight are added to the player's score.

Winning Conditions:

The game ends when :

-The timer runs out

-Innermost Circuit is filled

Blue Score : 0

Red Score : 0

The current player is

Blue Time : 30

Red Time : 30
